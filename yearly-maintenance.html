Seasonal Maintenance Tips

Keep your systems ready for West Texas weather

Spring Preparation

  • Schedule AC tune-up
  • Replace air filters
  • Clear outdoor unit debris
  • Test thermostat settings

Summer Readiness

  • Monitor energy usage
  • Keep vents unobstructed
  • Check for unusual noises
  • Maintain consistent temperatures

Fall Preparation

  • Schedule heating system check
  • Inspect ductwork
  • Test safety controls
  • Prepare for temperature drops

Winter Protection

  • Protect pipes from freezing
  • Monitor heating efficiency
  • Keep emergency contacts handy
  • Schedule water heater service
